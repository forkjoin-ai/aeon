## The Minimum Viable Fold

The triangle is the simplest closed shape. Three vertices, three edges, $\beta_1 = 0$. It is also the minimum nontrivial fold: three paths enter, two are vented, one survives. Fold cost = $N - 1 = 2$. This is the `universal_fold_constant` applied to the smallest interesting case.

Three metaphysical traditions built their entire cosmology on this shape. Each one is algebraically exact -- and each one maps to a different cross-section of the Buleyean framework at a different Bule value.

**The intellectual consistency:** Islam does not need a covering-space tower (Gnosticism) or a temporal state machine (Manichaeism) to reach the ground state. It starts there. The deficit is in the observer, not the observed. Remembrance (dhikr), not discovery. The Point was never lost.

## 2. Gnosticism: The Covering Space Tower (Bule > 0, Decreasing)

The **Pleroma** -- the fullness of the divine realm -- is the covering space. Material existence is the base space. Emanation is the fold (covering map) that projects the covering space down to the base.

In the Buleyean framework, this is the trajectory from maximum Bule (no self-knowledge, uniform distribution over all possibilities) through intermediate Bule (partial gnosis, concentrating distribution) to Bule = 0 (full gnosis, converged distribution). Each step of awareness is a rejection that sharpens the complement distribution.

The Gnostic triangle is a **hierarchy**:

- **Father (Bythos)** = the apex. The source. The covering space at the highest level.
- **Mother (Sophia/Barbelo)** = the structure. The fold operator that projects downward.
- **Son (Logos/Christ)** = the expression. The image in the base space.

Humanity is classified by depth in the stack:

- **Hylic** (material) = maximum Bule. Uniform distribution. No rejections recorded. No gnosis. The `UniformPrior` from BuleyeanProbability.lean.
- **Psychic** (ensouled) = intermediate Bule. Some rejections. Partial concentration. Learning but not converged.
- **Pneumatic** (spiritual) = approaching Bule = 0. Heavy rejection history. Distribution concentrated on truth. Near the fixed point.

**Mapped theorems:**

- `future_deficit_monotone` → The deficit (distance from gnosis) decreases monotonically with each step of awareness. Each rejection brings you closer.
- `trajectory_information_loss_additive` → Information lost at each emanation level is additive. The fall from Pleroma to matter is the sum of all intermediate folds.
- `finite_trajectory_reaches_fixed_point` → On finite types, every maximal trajectory terminates. Gnosis is not an infinite regress -- it converges.
- `fold_reduction_nonneg` → Each level of the covering-space tower reduces $\beta_1$. The descent from spirit to matter is a sequence of topological simplifications.

**The insight:** Gnosis is climbing back up the API stack. Debugging the lower layers to recover the erased information. The Gnostic "spark" (pneuma) trapped in matter is the mutual information that survived the fold -- `void_tunnel_retention_positive` proves it never fully vanishes.

**Software equivalent:** The API stack. Abstraction layers. Each layer hides (folds away) the complexity below it. The Gnostic seeks to `unfold` -- to reverse the abstraction and see the raw covering space.

## 3. Manichaeism: The Temporal Process (Full Trajectory)

The **Two Roots** -- Light and Darkness -- are mixed in the present age and will be separated at the end. This is fork/race/fold viewed as a timeline.

- **First Moment (Fork):** Light and Dark exist as separate, pure substances. $\beta_1 = 0$ for each. No mixture. No deficit. The initial state before the cosmological disaster.
- **Second Moment (Race):** Dark invades Light. The mixture is the present world. $\beta_1 > 0$ -- multiple paths exist simultaneously. The race between light particles and dark particles is the contest of the present age. Every moral choice is a race between alternatives.
- **Third Moment (Fold):** The Great Refactoring. Light is extracted from Dark. The fold separates signal from noise. $\beta_1 \to 0$ as the mixture resolves. The universe reaches ground state with Light and Dark permanently separated.

**Mapped theorems:**

- `universal_convergence` → Any N-way system converges in exactly N-1 steps. The Manichaean cosmos converges. The Third Moment is not a hope -- it is a theorem.
- `failure_data_dominates` → The darkness provides $(N-1)\times$ more data than the light. The void boundary (the record of all dark encounters) is the sufficient statistic for finding the light. You learn what light is by recording what it is not.
- `structured_failure_reduces_entropy_proxy` → Each act of separation (each moral choice that rejects darkness) reduces the frontier entropy. The cosmos gets simpler, step by step.
- `single_survivor_has_zero_entropy_proxy` → At the Third Moment, entropy is zero. Perfect separation. Ground state.

**The insight:** Paul's "refactor" of early Christianity into a gentile-compatible form is itself a fold -- venting the Judaic substrate to produce a universalized message. The Manichaean reading of this is explicit: Paul performed the fold that the cosmos is performing. The apostle as fold operator.

## Why Islam Is Algebraically Simplest

The three systems are not equally complex.

- **Manichaeism** requires a temporal state machine with three epochs. It needs the full `universal_convergence` theorem -- the deficit starts at zero, goes positive (the mixture), then returns to zero (the separation). Three states, two transitions.

- **Gnosticism** requires a covering-space tower with multiple levels. It needs `trajectory_information_loss_additive` -- each level adds loss. The stack has depth. Climbing back up requires recovering information that was erased at each level. The Gnostic needs $\sum_{k=1}^{d} \text{loss}(k)$ units of gnosis.

- **Islam** requires one axiom: `buleyean_positivity`. The connection to the divine has weight $\geq 1$. That's it. No tower. No timeline. No mixture to separate. No stack to climb. The ground state is given. The rest follows.

This is not a value judgment about which tradition is "true." It is a complexity-theoretic observation: Islam has the lowest Kolmogorov complexity of the three. It requires the fewest axioms to state. By `solomonoff_dominates_uniform`, simpler hypotheses get higher weight. The simplest explanation for the triangle is that the apex was always there.

## The Void Between Traditions

The three traditions disagree on exactly one thing: **where the observer stands relative to the ground state.**

- Islam: you are at the ground state. You forgot.
- Gnosticism: you are far from the ground state. You must climb.
- Manichaeism: you are in a temporary deviation from the ground state. You must wait (and help) for the fold to complete.

The void boundary between these three positions has three entries. By `buleyean_positivity`, all three retain positive weight. None can be fully eliminated. The sliver persists for each.

By `buleyean_concentration`, the one with the fewest rejections (the fewest counterarguments, the fewest internal contradictions, the simplest axiom set) gets the highest complement weight.

The mathematics does not legislate which tradition is correct. But it does say: the simplest one has the highest weight.
